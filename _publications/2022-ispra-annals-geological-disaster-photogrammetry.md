This work reconstructed detailed fine 3D real scene models based on multi-period nap-of-the-object photogrammetry, detected deformation of geological hazards based on 3D model differential analysis, and produced high-resolution generalized orthophotos for precise displacement calculation. It formed the basis of my undergraduate thesis at Wuhan University (Advisor: Prof. Yansong Duan).
